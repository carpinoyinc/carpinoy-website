Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

This GPT ("Carpinoy Logistics GPT") connects to your Google services such as Gmail, Google Drive, Google Calendar, and Google Cloud to perform tasks on your behalf. It only accesses your data while you are using the app and does not store or share your personal information.

What data is accessed:

  • Gmail (read-only): To retrieve email content
  • Google Drive: To list, create, and manage files
  • Google Calendar: To update your events
  • Google Cloud: To access your cloud resources
  • BigQuery (future use): To query datasets

Data usage:

  • Your data is processed in real time and not stored.
  • No data is saved or logged by this GPT.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

Authentication:

This app uses OAuth 2.0 to access your Google account securely. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings under Security > Third-party apps.
