Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Carpinoy Logistics Services exists not only to move cargo, but to open real business opportunities para sa mga Pilipinong gustong magsimula ng sarili nilang logistics negosyo sa tamang paraan.

🎯

Our Mission

The mission of Carpinoy Logistics Services is to deliver dependable, safe, and high-quality logistics solutions that connect warehouses, companies, and communities.

At its core, Carpinoy’s mission is to open doors of opportunity for more ordinary Filipinos who dream of starting their own logistics business. This mission is made possible through the Legit Complete Business Package Program, which provides every aspiring Filipino with a guided pathway to success.

Dependable logistics solutions
Consistent, organized, and on-time deliveries for partner companies.
Safe and compliant operations
Focus on LTFRB compliance, proper documentation, and professional handling of cargo.
Real opportunities for Filipinos
Tulay para sa mga gustong maging subcontractor at magkaroon ng sariling unit.
👁️

Our Vision

Our vision at Carpinoy Logistics Services is to expand our fleet and build a strong national network of partner companies — becoming the go-to trucking provider that businesses trust and recommend.

We aim to set higher industry standards through reliable service, operational excellence, and sustainable growth that benefits all partners. Beyond logistics, Carpinoy envisions helping more ordinary Filipinos achieve their dream of owning a logistics business through the Complete Business Package.

Stronger national presence
Growing fleet and coverage, connecting more warehouses and companies nationwide.
Trusted by businesses
To be the trucking partner that clients confidently recommend to others.
Higher industry standards
Better coordination, tracking, and support systems for day-to-day operations.
New generation of entrepreneurs
Providing guidance, support, and real opportunities to uplift lives and create successful Filipino entrepreneurs in the logistics industry.
