Public Explanation

Trusted Accredited Associates

Carpinoy Logistics Services formally introduces its Business Package Accredited Associates as part of its continuing advocacy to support individuals and entities who wish to participate in the logistics industry.

Nature of the Accredited Associate Program

Carpinoy Business Package Accredited Associates are exclusive, trusted, and authorized partners who are allowed to facilitate the entry of transport units strictly within the scope of the Authorized Car Dealership to which they are officially assigned.

No membership fee, joining fee, processing fee, or any similar charge is required to enter Carpinoy Logistics Services, provided that the onboarding is facilitated through Carpinoy Trusted Accredited Associates. Entry into Carpinoy is free of charge.

No Employer-Employee Relationship

Carpinoy Business Package Accredited Associates are NOT employees of Carpinoy Logistics Services. There exists no employer-employee relationship, whether express or implied. Associates operate independently, on their own discretion, methods, resources, and business judgment.

This program is extended purely as a business opportunity and a voluntary, non-compulsory free privilege.

No Guarantee of Income or Profit

Carpinoy does not provide any guarantee that participation will result in income, profit, or financial success. Outcomes depend on proper management, discipline, market conditions, and individual effort.

Purpose of the Program

The program is intended solely as a business-enabling platform — a free and optional avenue for individuals who aspire to start or expand a logistics-related enterprise without being burdened by entry fees or employment dependencies.

Issued for public information and guidance.
